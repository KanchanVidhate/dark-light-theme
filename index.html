....Dark - Light Mode....

Dark Theme:
Reduced Eye Strain: Dark themes are often preferred in low-light environments as they reduce the strain on the eyes, especially in dimly lit rooms or at night. The darker background causes less glare, making it easier on the eyes. Battery Savings.

Light Theme:
Readability: Light themes often provide better readability, particularly in well-lit environments or for users with certain visual impairments. Familiarity.
